Who We Are

Built by Engineers,
Trusted by Industry

RoboticsRemote was founded in 2014 by a team of robotics engineers, ex-NASA researchers, and telecom specialists with a single goal: make remote robotic operation as natural and reliable as being there in person.

Today we operate a global network of edge nodes that power teleoperation for clients in nuclear energy, aerospace, medicine, and underwater operations. Our team of 40+ engineers continues to push the boundaries of what's possible when human skill meets robotic precision.
